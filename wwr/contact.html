Employees

Jake Thompson – Lead Raft Guide

Jake is an experienced rafting guide with over a decade of navigating whitewater rapids. He’s known for his calm demeanor under pressure and his ability to keep guests safe while ensuring they have a thrilling adventure. When he's not on the river, he enjoys rock climbing and teaching water rescue courses.

Marcus Holloway – Equipment and Safety Specialist

Marcus is responsible for maintaining and inspecting all rafting gear, from life jackets to paddles and rafts. A former mechanical engineer, he brings a meticulous eye to equipment safety, ensuring that everything meets top industry standards. When he’s not working, he enjoys building custom kayaks and testing them on nearby rivers.

Samantha Delgado – Customer Experience Manager

Samantha is the friendly face behind the company’s customer service and trip coordination. She ensures that every guest has a seamless experience from booking to post-trip feedback. With a background in outdoor tourism and hospitality, she’s passionate about connecting people with nature and making every adventure unforgettable.
